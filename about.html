Our Story

Where Elegance
Meets Purposeful
Design

Born from Singapore's finest construction heritage, OliveOak is where precision meets artistry. We craft bespoke residential interiors not merely spaces, but reflections of the lives lived within them.

Every project is an exercise in restraint and excellence. We curate materials, light, and form with deliberate intent delivering interiors that endure not just in craftsmanship, but in meaning.

Our Philosophy

Three Pillars of
Exceptional Design

Every space we create is built upon these core design principles

01

Function First

Every room has a role. We design with intention, ensuring each corner serves your lifestyle with elegance, never imposing style over function. Your home should work as beautifully as it looks.

02

Timeless Craft

Trends fade, craftsmanship endures. We curate palettes, materials, and forms that transcend time, creating spaces that remain beautiful decades into the future. Quality over fleeting fashion, always.

03

Singapore Precision

Born from TEV Contractors' Singapore heritage, every detail is executed with the discipline and quality benchmarks that define world-class design. Precision planning, transparent processes, flawless delivery.
