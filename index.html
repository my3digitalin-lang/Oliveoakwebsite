About OliveOak

Where Spaces Inspire,
and Interior Design
Comes Alive

Olive Oak is the signature interior design identity of TEV Contractors, Singapore. With a strong heritage in construction and a clear vision for artistry, we deliver bespoke interior design solutions that embody elegance, functionality, and timeless sophistication.

Every interior design project we undertake is a narrative of refined living and purposeful design crafted for those who value distinction, quality, and enduring aesthetics.
